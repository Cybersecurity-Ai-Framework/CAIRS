# Executive Cyber Reporting

Executive Cyber Reporting defines how CAIF translates validated technical risk into business, board, and operational decision context.

## Scope

- Risk narratives grounded in evidence.
- Executive scoring and prioritization language.
- Trend, exposure, and remediation reporting.
- Standards for AI-generated security reports.

## Contribution status

Open for security leaders, GRC contributors, and executive reporting practitioners.
