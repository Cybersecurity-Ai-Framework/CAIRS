# Exposure Intelligence

Exposure Intelligence focuses on discovering, classifying, and prioritizing reachable assets, services, identities, and attack paths.

## Scope

- Internet-facing asset discovery.
- Cloud, API, identity, and application exposure mapping.
- Attack surface context for vulnerability validation.
- Evidence requirements for exposure claims.

## Contribution status

Open for researchers building repeatable exposure mapping standards.
