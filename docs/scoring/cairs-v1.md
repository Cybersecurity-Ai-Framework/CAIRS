# CAIRS v1.0

CAIRS is the Cybersecurity AI Risk Score. It is a proposed scoring model for communicating security risk with evidence quality, exploitability, exposure, and business impact in one explainable view.

## MVP dimensions

- Exploitability
- Exposure
- Business Impact
- Evidence Quality
- Attack Chain Potential
- Remediation Complexity

The homepage includes a static CAIRS example. The public calculator now starts with CAIF-CVSS because it has a clearer metric structure for community review. CAIRS and CAIF-CVSS can later converge if contributors approve a shared scoring model.
